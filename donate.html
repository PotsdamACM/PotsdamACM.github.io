Donate

When you donate, we'll post your generous contribution to this page.

How is my Donation Used?

When the money you donate is used, you'll be able to see exactly it is going and how you are helping to support and grow the SUNY Potsdam ACM. If you'd like, you can specify where you would like your money to go when you donate.

How to Donate

Online

  1. Donate online by clicking here.
  2. Fill in “SGA - ACM" for the Specific Program / Activity field. Do not check the “Greatest needs of the college” box.
  3. Leave a comment if you would like and we’ll post it here.
  4. Fill in the remainder of the fields with your information.
  5. Fill in the amount of money you would like to donate in the “Gift Amount” box at the bottom of the page.
  6. When asked to “Select Method of Payment”, click the continue button.
  7. Enter your card information and click the continue button.
  8. Review your payment option and click “Submit Gift”.
  9. We will receive email notifcation and then we will post you to our site.

By Check

  1. Please make the check payable to Potsdam College Foundation with the memo of ACM - SGA.
  2. Send the check to Potsdam College Foundation, Inc. at 44 Pierrepont Ave., Potsdam, NY 13676-2294, Raymond Hall 507.
  3. Email us your comment you'd like to leave and we'll post you to the site.

Need Help?

If you need help making your gift, please contact Vicky Perry in the Foundation office at 315-267-3054. General questions about supporting the ACM should be directed to Jesse at peplinjj199@potsdam.edu.

Other Donations

If you want to donate hardware or anything else to the ACM, simply talk to any one of the ACM officers.

The UPE is the only honorary society whose membership consists of undergraduate and graduate students in CIS disciplines and recognized by ACM and the IEEE-CS. They provide scholarship awards to students of the ACM and supporters of the association. They also provide cash awards to chapters that send representatives to it's annual conventions and plaques to teams that compete at the ACM Programming contest. UPE presents the ABACUS Award - the most prestigious award presented to an individual who, over several years, has provided extensive support and leadership for student-related activities in the Computing and Information Disciplines.
