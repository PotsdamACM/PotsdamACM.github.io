Doug Crescenzi Guest Speaking

perm_contact_calendarSeptember 10, 2016

av_timer12:00 Noon

location_onDunn Hall 102

Doug Crescenzi Guest Speaking

A guest speaker from Syracuse, NY is coming to give a talk to students in SUNY Potsdam. Bio: Doug, a PhD dropout, enjoys building products and companies which address challenging problems. In May 2013, Doug co-founded Lono, a connected device company producing intelligent, WiFi-enabled sprinkler controllers. In January 2013, Doug co-founded Hack Upstate. Hack Upstate’s mission is to unite and facilitate collaboration among the greater Upstate New York technology community. In pursuit, Hack Upstate organize hackathons, offers web and mobile development classes, facilitate talks and lectures, and ultimately helps align Upstate tech talent with promising employment opportunities. To date, Hack Upstate has built a growing network comprised of thousands of Upstate New York engineers and nearly a hundred technology employers.
